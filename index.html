About me

Greetings! I am currently on an exhilarating journey as a Full Stack Developer in training at Emerging Digital Academy, every moment is a step toward mastering the art of coding. Emerging Digital Academy is a 800+ hour, 20 week coding bootcamp that teaches FullStack Javascript and professional development.I thrive on the challenge of identifying problems and devising creative solutions, driven by the belief that technology can be a force for positive change. I'm fueled by a deep passion for people and culture.

Having explored various corners of the globe as a dedicated world traveler, I've had the privilege of immersing myself in diverse cultures, fostering connections with individuals from all walks of life. These experiences have instilled in me a unique perspective and a flexible mindset, invaluable assets when it comes to problem-solving. Beyond the realm of coding, my aspiration is to contribute to making the world a better place by actively participating in community initiatives. In my spare time, you'll find me lost in the pages of fantasy novels, cherishing moments with friends and family, and capturing the beauty of the world through my lens.
